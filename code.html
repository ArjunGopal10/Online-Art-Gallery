Artists

Leonardo Da Vinci

Country: Italy

Biography: Leonardo di ser Piero da Vinci was an Italian polymath of the High Renaissance who was active as a painter, draughtsman, engineer, scientist, theorist, sculptor, and architect.

Vincent Van Gogh

Country: Netherlands

Biography: Vincent Willem van Gogh was a Dutch Post-Impressionist painter who is among the most famous and influential figures in the history of Western art. In just over a decade he created approximately 2100 artworks, including around 860 oil paintings, most of them in the last two years of his life.

Pablo Picasso

Country: French

Biography:Pablo Ruiz Picasso was a Spanish painter, sculptor, printmaker, ceramicist and theatre designer who spent most of his adult life in France.

James Whistler

Country: America

Biography: James Abbott McNeill Whistler RBA was an American painter in oils and watercolor, and printmaker, active during the American Gilded Age and based primarily in the United Kingdom. He eschewed sentimentality and moral allusion in painting and was a leading proponent of the credo "art for art's sake".

Franz Marc

Country: Germany

Biography: Franz Moritz Wilhelm Marc was a German painter and printmaker, one of the key figures of German Expressionism. He was a founding member of Der Blaue Reiter, a journal whose name later became synonymous with the circle of artists collaborating in it. His mature works mostly are animals, and are known for bright colors.

About Us

Welcome to Art Gallery, your gateway to a world of artistic expression. We are passionate about showcasing the finest artworks and connecting art lovers with talented artists from all over the globe. Our gallery is not just a place to admire art; it's a space where imagination comes to life. We invite you to explore the beauty, creativity, and stories behind each masterpiece, created by artists who pour their hearts into their work. Founded in 2023, Art Gallery has been dedicated to promoting art and culture. With numerous successful exhibitions and an ever-growing collection, we continue to celebrate the brilliance of human creativity. Join us on this journey of discovery and appreciation, and let art inspire your soul.
